# A Gruesome Sight
When you finally reach the village, you find it embroiled in a full-pitched battle. A mass of Fauns appear to be pushing the Firbolg villagers up against the cliffside by the ritual.

The kids with you freak out, and Nutsy turns to you with tears in his eyes. *"What's happening? Where did all these demons come from?"* He spies his mother grappling with a large Faun woman, face half buried under her massive breasts. *"Mommy!"* he cries. His mother turns, locking eyes with Nutsy, then turns to you and scowls. While she's distracted, the Faun above her rears back and smashes her with her tits.
## Enemy Stat Blocks:
### Adult villager stat blocks (cult adepts):

| Difficulty | Thresholds | HP  | Stress | ATK | Weapon                              |
| ---------- | ---------- | --- | ------ | --- | ----------------------------------- |
| 14         | 9/18       | 4   | 6      | +2  | Rune covered rod - far, 2d4+3 magic |
- Features:
	- Enervating blast - action, spend fear to make standard attack against a target within range. On success, target must mark a stress.
	- Shroud of the fallen - action, mark a stress to wrap an ally within close range in a shroud of protection until the adept marks their last HP. While protected, the target has resistance (halve incoming damage) to all damage.
	- Shadow shackles - action, spend a fear and choose a point within far range. All targets within close range of that point are restrained in smoky chains until they break free with a successful strength or instinct roll. A target restrained by this feature must spend a hope to make an action roll.
	- Fear is fuel - reaction, twice per scene, when a PC rolls a failure with fear, clear a stress.
