# A Curious Exit
Picking up from where we left off last:

Aurum, DK, Griffin, Paulie, The Captain, and four Firbolg children are running desperately away from a powerful foe. Originally appearing as an eagle, this monster obliterated with a chain lightning most of the Fauns attempting to make the coffee bean and living sacrifice to Lady Ylime. Lady Dolor and her closest advisor were chased away from the sacrificial cliff back into their village, the ritual remaining incomplete. You were able to recover one glass component for the evaporator from the ritual site, and are now running for your lives from the enraged eagle.

The children (minus the one with peg legs) were starting to fall behind, their tiny legs unable to keep up with the pace set by fully grown adventurers. Aurum, seeing the children in trouble, shouted for the rest of the group to keep running while he turned and delivered a mighty blow straight to the face of the oncoming eagle. It manages to scrape Aurum with its talons before being blasted backwards by the force of his strike. As the eagle tumbled backwards, it seemed to be forcefully transformed. What stood in the settling dust was a figure with enormous breasts and a gigantic penis, and the large, curving horns of fauns, but the long, drooping ears of a Firbolg. She snarled at you, and said, *"You're dead meat."*

> [!faq]- Question about Tinafer's Appearance (Easy Investigation)
> The strange creature is wearing gloves with glowing fingertips of changing colors, and is constantly flexing and flourishing her fingers.

She then cocks her head, as if listening to something. With a smirk, she turns back to Aurum and says, *"Lucky you. You get to live a little longer."* She jumps and transforms into an eagle again, spreading her wings so that she is caught by the wind and rockets back towards the site of the massacre.

As you make your way back up the tower, something feels different from the last time you were here, just an hour or two before.

> [!info] Roll Time!
> Everyone roll a perception (Instinct) check for me. Meet or beat 15.

Failure:
- You hear the loud grinding of something mechanical, possibly gears and pulleys.
Success:
- You also hear the squeaking of hundreds and hundreds of rats charging down the stairs towards you.

> [!info] Roll Time!
> Everyone roll an Agility or Strength check for me. Those of you who succeeded in your perception check, add a 1d6 advantage die to your roll. Meet or beat 18.

Failure:
- You are swept off your feet and carried part of the way down the stairs. Take 1d4 damage from falling and 1d4 damage from being partially submerged under the tide of rats.
Success:
- Describe how you manage to stay on your feet or are otherwise unaffected by the surge of rodents.

As you reach the top of the tower, you open the door to find that the water gates have been opened. A massive gap stretches between where you stand and the platform on the other tower. 

While you deliberate on what to do, we will quickly jump to the Firbolg village just a few hours ago. Othello awake to find themselves laying in the mud of an empty animal pen. They are alone, and all is silent. **Allow them to role play a bit.** Suddenly, a door is flung open. *"Nutsy, where the devil are you?"* screams a Firbolg woman. She charges towards you, a look of disgust on her face. *"What did you devil spies do with my Nutsy? Your friends had better not have taken him to the other crescent, or I promise you I'll smash your tiny peckers in!"*

Allow her to exposition dump a bit to them in character, before pushing them to go look for Nutsy and the rest of the crew on the devil's crescent. They pull up to the tower and see the rest of the crew arguing about what to do. (Prompt players to roleplay being in an argument)
