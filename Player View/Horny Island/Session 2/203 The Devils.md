# It Ain't Easy Being Green
Large towers sit on either side of the gate, with one door leading to a spiral staircase up to the top of the gate. The stairs are narrow and dark and cold, and seem to go on forever. If the front person doesn't verbally say they hold something in front of their face, they will run into some spider webs and it will be gross.

The bridge across the water gate is less of a bridge and more of an incidental path along the top of the gate. There are no railings on either side of the walkway, and it is thin enough that only one person may walk abreast. If you look down the side, you see on your left a decent drop into the ocean. On your right is a massive drop, to a much lower water level in the bay. Falling on the right would spell certain death for any unlucky enough to hit the water.

You cross the bridge uneventfully. Apparently the Devils are equally too busy with their own preparations for El Dia del Viento to guard such an old, unused path. Nutsy wants to head towards the ritual site to set fire to their coffee beans, but you may also go towards the village as well if you wish, or explore this side of the island in general.

## The Ritual:
As you approach the cliffs overlooking the bay, you spy a large tower of barrels identical to the one on the other crescent. The one difference is a large pole in the middle of the barrels, with two dark shapes hanging from it. There is a small group of people milling about around the barrels, preparing for the ritual.

Tit smacking/dick smacking on each respective crescent.

Ended by Tinafer chasing them back to the tower bridge. Most of the fauns are dead from her chain lightning. She has revealed her true form after being smacked by Aurum. They have recovered one of the glass components of the evaporator. They also learned Lady Dolor's name and heard Father Pena mention his wife.
