# A Stressful Time of the Month
At dawn on the next day, you awake to the sounds of hustle and bustle around the Firbolg village. People of all ages and sizes are carrying bananas in great handfuls to the beach in the direction of  your ship. Those who aren't carrying bananas are pushing carts full of barrels in the other direction, towards the bay.

A Navy ship is beached there to buy bananas in exchange for food and trade goods.

Items that can be found on the beach within the wreckage:
- Woven net
- Empty chest
	- Handful of gold
	- 3 Minor health potions
	- 3 Minor stamina potions
- Some broken evaporator parts

You see the child from the night before, Nutsy, run up to you. *"Hey ugly!"* he says to the group in general. *"You have weapons. Real weapons. Wanna kill some devils?"*

Nutsy and a few other children are organizing a raid on the other side of the island. They are going with or without you, but thought that "some more fire power" on their side wouldn't hurt.

The bridge is unguarded right now, since all the adults are helping with the preparations.
