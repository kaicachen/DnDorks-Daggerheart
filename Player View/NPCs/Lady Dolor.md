## Overview:
- Leader of the local chapter of the [[Fauna Federalis]]
- Last of the first generation of Faun slaves on [[Kai_DM_Notes/Horny Island/Session 2/201 Horny Island|Horny Island]], remembers life before slavery
- "You have it so easy these days" type of boomer
- Whiny, obnoxious
- Greedy bootlicker
- Very old
## Voice Prompt:
- Reedsy, whiny, high pitched, "ohhh my!"
## Wants:
- To convince the Fauns that its not fair how easy they have it these days, and how they need to work harder to meet their quotas
- To remind the Fauns of how hard she had it growing up and how lucky they are to be able to work for food
- To spite her ex-lover [[Player View/NPCs/Father Pena]]
## Fears:
- Being usurped from what little power she holds
- The devils on the other half of the island and the trickery they commit
## General:
- Daughter of the previous leader, comes from a long line of village elders and leaders (nepo baby)
- Distantly related to [[Player View/NPCs/Father Pena]] (unknown to her)
- Used to have a love affair with [[Player View/NPCs/Father Pena]], secret but known to [[Player View/NPCs/Figaro & Tinafer]], who are their affair children
