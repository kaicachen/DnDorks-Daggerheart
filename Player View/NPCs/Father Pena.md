## Overview:
- Leader of the local chapter of the [[Firbolgs for Freedom]]
- Last of the first generation of Firbolg slaves on [[Kai_DM_Notes/Horny Island/Session 2/201 Horny Island|Horny Island]], remembers life before slavery
- Penis that is so long and thick that it drags along the ground behind him, leaving the impression of a tail in the sand
- "Back in my day" type of boomer
- Prideful bootlicker
- Very old
## Voice Prompt:
- Breathy, higher pitched "oh, aye"
## Wants:
- To convince the Firbolgs that they just need to pull themselves up by their bootstraps and meet their quotas and life will get better
- To remind the Firbolg masses of how great life is like outside of slavery, and how if they're just better slaves then they'll be set free one day
## Fears:
- Being usurped from what little power he holds
- The devils on the other half of the island and the trickery they commit
## General:
- Son of the previous leader, comes from a long line of village elders and leaders (nepo baby)
- Distantly related to [[Player View/NPCs/Lady Dolor]] (unknown to him)
- Used to have a love affair with [[Player View/NPCs/Lady Dolor]], secret but known to [[Player View/NPCs/Figaro & Tinafer]], who are their affair children
